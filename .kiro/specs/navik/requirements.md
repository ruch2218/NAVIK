# NAVIK – Requirements Specification

## 1. Overview
NAVIK is a voice-first, multilingual AI assistant designed for Bharat. It helps citizens—especially in rural and semi-urban areas—access government services, healthcare information, and agricultural advisories using simple voice or text interactions in Indian languages.

## 2. Problem Statement
Many citizens face difficulties accessing government schemes and public services due to:
- Language barriers
- Low digital literacy
- Complex government portals
- Dependence on intermediaries

NAVIK aims to bridge this gap by providing simple, verified, and voice-based assistance.

## 3. Target Users
- Rural and semi-urban citizens
- Farmers
- Elderly users
- Low-literacy users
- First-time smartphone users

## 4. Functional Requirements
- The system shall support voice-based interaction.
- The system shall support multiple Indian languages.
- The system shall provide verified information about government schemes.
- The system shall provide healthcare guidance and agricultural advisories.
- The system shall guide users step-by-step to access services.
- The system shall allow both voice and text input.

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements
- The system should be easy to use and intuitive.
- Responses should be simple and easy to understand.
- The system should be scalable for future services.
- The system should ensure information accuracy.
- The system should be reliable with low response time.

## 6. Constraints
- Internet connectivity may be limited in rural areas.
- Users may have low-end devices.
- Language diversity across regions.

## 7. Assumptions
- Users have access to a basic smartphone.
- Government data sources are available for integration.

## 8. Success Criteria
- Reduced dependency on intermediaries.
- Improved access to government services.
- Increased digital inclusion.
